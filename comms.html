important notes

Frankenmodeling Approach: My modules are created using existing Project Diva models. I may model small accessories or hair modifications, but the core structure uses game assets. This keeps costs affordable while maintaining quality.
Design Matching: Due to the frankenmodeling approach, modules may not match your reference design exactly. I work to get as close as possible while working within the constraints of available assets.
Manual Modeling: I do not provide full manual modeling services. If you need completely custom models, I recommend contacting creators like Mekaio, Sangabc, Royer, Nada, and others in the PDM2 or MM+M servers.
